To My Beautiful (and Slightly Weird) Girlfriend,

Happy Girlfriends Day to the person who puts up with my nonsense daily and will still choose to kiss me goodnight (even when I steal all the blankets and snore like a chainsaw).

I don’t know how I got so lucky, maybe you lost a bet?, but somehow, you’re stuck with me, and I’m thrilled about it. You’re my favorite human, even when you hog the fries, take 500 selfies before picking one, or pretend not to laugh at my terrible jokes (we both know you think I’m hilarious).

Life with you is the best adventure, full of love, laughter, and way too many debates. Thank you for being my partner-in-crime, my personal comedian (when you’re not laughing at me), and the only person I’d willingly share my dessert with.

Here’s to more ridiculous moments, inside jokes that make no sense to anyone else, and a lifetime of you pretending not to be amused by my antics. I love you more than my phone battery… and that’s saying a lot.

Forever your goofball,
your Programmer (stark)
